Starting with a new system can sometimes feel overwhelming, but with **eSwiftSchool**, the process is designed to be smooth and straightforward.  

Whether you’re a teacher, administrator, or school owner, this guide will help you take your first steps.

<!-- truncate -->

### 📝 Step 1: Log in to Your Dashboard
Go to your **school portal** *(for example: `brightfuture.eswiftschool.com`)*.  

On the **Staff Login Page**, enter your **email** and **password**.  
Once logged in, you will arrive at your **school’s dashboard** — the central place to manage classes, staff, students, timetables, and reports.  

👉 If you don’t yet have a school portal or need to create one, please [read about how to set up your portal here](/docs/admin/intro).

---

### 👩‍🏫 Step 2: Add Your Students and Staff
Quickly add students and staff members so everyone is connected in one place.  
Academic staff can be linked directly to their classes, while admin staff can help manage school-wide operations.

---

### 📚 Step 3: Create Classes and Subjects
Organize your school structure by setting up classes, subjects, and timetables.  
This ensures students and teachers are assigned correctly, and reports are generated automatically.

---

### 📊 Step 4: Track Attendance and Performance
With just a few clicks, you can mark attendance and record performance.  
Reports are automatically updated, saving you time and effort.
