# Getting Started

Welcome to **eSwiftSchool**!  
This guide will walk you through **creating your school portal** and completing the **onboarding setup** for the first time.

---

## Step 1 - Creating Your School Portal

1. **Go to eSwiftSchool**
   - Open your browser and visit **[www.eswiftschool.com](https://www.eswiftschool.com)**.
   - Click the **Create Your School Account** button.

2. **Fill in Your School Details**
   - **Name of School** — Full name as you want it displayed.
   - **School Type** — Day School, Boarding School, or Combined.
   - **Subdomain** — Your school’s unique web address inside eSwiftSchool *(e.g., `brightfuture.eswiftschool.com`)*.

     <details>
     <summary>📝 What is a Subdomain?</summary>

     Your **Subdomain** is the **short name** that will be part of your school’s eSwiftSchool web address.  

     ⚠️ **Important:** Only type the short name — *do not* include “.eswiftschool.com” or “https://”.

     **Examples:**
     - `greenfield` → **greenfield.eswiftschool.com**  
     - `brightacademy` → **brightacademy.eswiftschool.com**

     💡 **Tip:**  
     - Keep it short and easy to remember.  
     - Avoid spaces and special characters.
     </details>

   - **Country** — Your school’s country.
   - **School Address** — Your school’s full address.

3. **Enter Your Admin Details**
   - **Admin's Full Name** — First and last name.
   - **Admin's Email** — Used for login.
   - **Password** — At least 6 characters, mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
   - **Confirm Password** — Re-enter the password.

4. **Agree & Submit**
   - Tick the checkbox for **Terms & Privacy Policy**.
   - Click **Finish**.

---

## Step 2 - Onboarding

After logging in for the first time, eSwiftSchool will guide you through your **initial setup**:

1. **Verify your Email** — Check your inbox for a verification link from eSwiftSchool. Click it to confirm your email.
2. **Add Classes** — Create at least one class to start enrolling students.
3. **Add Staff** — Invite teachers and admins.
4. **Set Up Fee Structure** *(optional)* — Define school fees for finance tracking.

> ✅ You can skip any step now and complete it later from your admin dashboard.

**Once Onboarding is complete:**
- You’ll land on your **Admin Dashboard**.
- From here, you can manage **students, staff, classes, attendance, and finances**.
